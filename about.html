Hey folks! I'm Brody, a Computer Science student at NC State and President of Rooted, an NC State student-run Christian campus ministry. My undergraduate degree was focussed mostly on cybersecurity and software engineering, so grad school has been a blast because I've gotten to learn about lots of things I missed in undergrad such as data mining (this is my plug for CSC 522)!

In addition to school, I also work as a Lab Admin for E 115 which basically means I'm here to help students, TAs, and the Course Instructors effectively get done what needs to be done. My favorite part of being a Lab Admin though are my office hours as I get to interact with students and thus meet a lot of cool people; if you're an E 115 student, feel free to drop by my office hours - either for help or just to talk!

In my free time, I love to hike, sleep, play board games, listen to music, and and study the Bible - I am currently going through OT/NT in a year plan and an in-depth study of Colossians! I also serve in Rooted, an NC State campus ministry organization and in my local church. All of this - everything - by God's grace.
